# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from a Superstore dataset to uncover insights into sales performance, profitability, regional trends, and product categories. The analysis was performed using Python for data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA), and Power BI for interactive dashboard development.

## Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance across categories and regions.
* Identify top-performing states and business segments.
* Evaluate the impact of discounts on profitability.
* Develop an interactive dashboard to support business decision-making.

## Key Insights

### Category Performance

* Technology generated the highest sales revenue ($836K).
* Technology was also the most profitable category ($145K profit).
* Furniture generated strong sales but relatively low profit, indicating lower margins.

### Regional Performance

* West region recorded the highest sales ($725K).
* East region ranked second with $678K in sales.
* South region showed the lowest sales performance.

### State Performance

Top-performing states:

1. California
2. New York
3. Texas
4. Washington
5. Pennsylvania

### Business Recommendations

* Increase investment in Technology products due to strong profitability.
* Review Furniture pricing and discount strategies to improve margins.
* Expand marketing efforts in the South region to drive growth.
* Prioritize inventory planning for California and New York.
